Mushroom Pork Chops

Description

Quick and easy, but very delicious. One of my family's favorites served over brown rice.

Cooking Information

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 30 mins
  • Total: 40 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 servings

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 210 calories; protein 23.6g; carbohydrates 9.6g; fat 8.5g; cholesterol 64.9mg; sodium 924.3mg.

Ingredients

  • 4 pork chops
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup

Directions

  1. Step 1
  2. Season pork chops with salt, pepper, and garlic salt to taste.
  3. Step 2
  4. In a large skillet, brown the chops over medium-high heat. Add the onion and mushrooms, and saute for one minute. Pour cream of mushroom soup over chops. Cover skillet, and reduce temperature to medium-low. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes, or until chops are cooked through.
