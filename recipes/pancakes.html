Pancakes

Description

Easy and quick to make. One of the best pankcake recipes around!

Cooking Information

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 15mins
  • Total: 20mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 Servings

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 158 calories; protein 4.5g; carbohydrates 21.7g; fat 5.9g; cholesterol 37.7mg; sodium 503.6mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions

  1. Step 1
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Step 2
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
