Scrambled Eggs!

Description

There is more to just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you.

Cooking Information

  • Prep: 2 min
  • Cook: 4 min
  • Total: 6 min
  • Servings: 1
  • Yield: 1 Serving

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 210 calories; protein 12.7g; carbohydrates 1g; fat 17.4g; cholesterol 373.7mg; sodium 210.4mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspon mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon water (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon margarine or butter
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1
  2. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist. Do not over cook. Never add salt or pepper until eggs are on plate, but these are also good without.
